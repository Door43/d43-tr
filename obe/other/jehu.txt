====== Jehu ======

===== Facts: =====

Jehu was king of Israel for twenty-eight years,   (842-825 BC), a descendent of Jehosphaphat.

  * He came to the throne after the murder of Joram through the inspiration of the prophet Elisha.
  * He felt commissioned to wipe out the house of Ahab which he eventually accomplished.
  * He slew both Joram, King of Israel and Ahaziah, King of Judah, and the queen mother, Jezebel.
