====== Haggai ======

===== Facts: =====

Haggai was one of Israel's Old Testament minor prophets after they had returned back to their land from captivity.

  * Haggai was a prophet during the post-exilic period of the Jews, ministering during the second temple rebuild.
  * Haggai's message to Israel was to start rebuilding the temple again since they had stopped the work.
