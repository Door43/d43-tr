====== Joram ======

===== Facts: =====

A King of Israel; son of Ahab and Jezebel.

  * Joram reigned at the same time as Jehoram King of Judah, which can cause confusion between the two.
  * Joram King of Israel was a godless man who followed the ways of Jeroboam son of Nebat, causing Israel to sin.
