====== Kedesh ======

===== Facts: =====

An Old Testament Canaanite citadel located at the North end of Israel's territory.

  * Under the leadership of Joshua Israel took Kadesh from the Canaanites
  * Kadesh was given to the tribe of Naphtali, and was set apart as a Levitical city for the priests and a city of refuge.
