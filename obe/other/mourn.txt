====== mourn, mourning ======

===== Facts: =====

To mourn is to express profound grief over the death of someone.

  * In Biblical times mourning was a higher developed and ritualized act which included weeping, wailing and laments.
  * Professional mourners, primarily women, could be hired to mourn from the time of death until well after entombment.
  * The typical period of mourning was seven days, but could last as long as thirty days (Moses and Aaron) or seventy days (Jacob).
