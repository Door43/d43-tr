====== Jehoiakim ======

===== Facts: =====

Originally named Eliakim, Jehoiakim was King of Judah starting approximately 608 B.C.

  * Jehoiakim served as a vassal to Necho Pharoah of Egypt, paying heavy tribute tax to Egypt.
  * Jehoiakim was a godless King who led Judah away from Yahweh. Jeremiah the prophet spoke against him during his prophecies.
