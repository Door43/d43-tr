====== Jezreel ======

===== Definition: =====

A town in Issachar's territory where the Kings of Israel often took residence.

  * The Prophet Elijah met the wicked King Ahab in Jezreel and there prophesied against him.
  * Jezreel is one of the Western points in the valley of Megiddo. (Har-Megiddon)
