====== Joash ======

===== Facts: =====

The same as Jehoash, 9th King of Juda around 852 B.C.

  * As a child Joash was hidden from the murderous Queen Athaliah until age six when he was set up as King of Judah.
  * The Prophet Elisha foretold that Joash would defeat the Syrian King Ben-Hadad, which he successfully did.
