====== Jezebel ======

===== Facts: =====

The wicked wife of Ahab King of Israel.

  * Jezebel's most infamous act was having an innocent man named Naboth killed so Ahab could steal his land.
  * The prophet Elijah prophesied against Jezebel and she received a violent death due to her wicked acts.
