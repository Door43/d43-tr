====== interpret ======

===== Facts: =====

In many Old Testament references this term refers to the understanding and explaining of dreams.

  * When the King of Babylon had dreams God gave Daniel the ability to understand them and to interpret them, thus giving the meaning.
  * To interpret is the action of explaining the dream or dreams; interpretation refers to that meaning which was discovered and recorderd.
  * In the Old Testament dream interpretations were often prophetic predictions given by God.
