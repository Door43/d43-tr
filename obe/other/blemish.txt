====== blemish ======

===== Facts: =====

A blemish is a fault, defect, or imperfection in an animate or inanimate objet.

  * Isarelites were instructed  by God to offer a pure or unblemished animal for certain sacrifices. (Ezekial 43:22)
  * Jesus Christ is the sacrifice without blemish.  (Hebrews 9:14)
