====== Josiah ======

===== Facts: =====

A Godly King of Judah beginning approximately 640 B.C.

  * During Josiah's reign many reforms were instituted to bring Judah back to Yahweh.
  * Josiah ordered Hilkiah the high priest to rebuild the Temple of the Lord. During this rebuild the books of the law were found.
