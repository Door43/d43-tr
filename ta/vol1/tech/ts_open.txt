===== Çeviri Projesinin Açılması =====

This module answers the question: //translationStudio’da bir çeviri projesini nasıl açabilirim?​//

Before you start this module have you learned about:

  * //[[:tr:ta:vol1:tech:ts_install|Install translationStudio on an Android Device]]//

==== İlk Kez Kullanım ====

translationStudio’yu ilk kez kullandığınızda uygulama sizi hedef dili uyarlamaya teşvik edecek, sonra **«Çeviri Projelerim»** menüsüne dönecek. Aksi hâlde siz **Çeviri Projelerim Menüsünden ** başlayacaksınız.

==== Complete these steps to open a Translation Project ====

From the "My Translation Projects" Menu:
  - Tap on the + in the blue circle icon located at the top right of the screen.
  - Choose the target language 
    - Tap on the language 
    - Scroll to find the language
  - Tap on to choose either the Bible:NT, Bible:OT, or Open Bible Stories
    - Tap on a book of the Bible or Open Bible Stories opens on story 1, The Creation.
  - Tap on a Source Language to choose one or more languages (See: [[tr:ta:vol1:tech:ts_select]] for more information)
From the edit screen:
  - Tap on 3 dots located at bottom left of the screen.
  - Choose 'My Translations' from the menu.
  - Follow the directions above.
