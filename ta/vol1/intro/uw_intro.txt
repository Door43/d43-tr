==== Neden ====

İsa öğrencilerine, her etnik grubu öğrencileri olarak yetiştirmelerini emretti:

<blockquote>
« İsa yanlarına gelip kendilerine şunları söyledi: «Gökte ve yeryüzünde bütün yetki bana verildi. Bu nedenle gidin, bütün ulusları öğrencilerim olarak yetiştirin; onları Baba, Oğul ve Kutsal Ruh’un adıyla vaftiz edin; size buyurduğum her şeye uymayı onlara öğretin. İşte ben, dünyanın sonuna dek her an sizinle birlikteyim.» (Matta 28:18-20)</blockquote>

Bize bir gün HER dil grubunun temsilcilerinin cennette olacağı vaadi verilmiştir:

<blockquote>«Bundan sonra gördüm ki, her ulustan, her oymaktan, her halktan, her dilden oluşan, kimsenin sayamayacağı kadar büyük bir kalabalık tahtın ve Kuzu’nun önünde duruyordu.» (Vahiy 7:9)</blockquote>

Her insanın Tanrı’nın sözünü öz ana dilinde anlaması çok önemlidir.

<blockquote>«Demek ki iman, haberi duymakla, duymak da Mesih’le ilgili sözün yayılmasıyla olur.» (Romalılara 10:17)</blockquote>

Peki, yaptığımızı neden yapıyoruz?

UnfordingWord projesinin varlığının sebebi, her etnik grubun kendi dilinde Tanrı’nın sözüne sahip olmayı hak etmesindendir. Bu amaçla **dünyanın her dilinde kısıtlamasız Kutsal Kitap içeriğini** sağlamaya yardım etmek yoluyla bütün milletleri İsa’nın öğrencisi olarak yetiştirmeye çalışıyoruz.

==== How ====

How do we accomplish our goal of **unrestricted biblical content in every language**?

   * The unfoldingWord Network - By partnering with other like-minded organizations (see [[http://www.unfoldingword.org|www.unfoldingword.org]] )
  * [[:tr:ta:vol1:intro:statement_of_faith|Statement of Faith]] - By working with people and organizations who have the same beliefs
  * [[:tr:ta:vol1:intro:translation_guidelines|Translation Guidelines]] - By using a common translation theory
  * [[:tr:ta:vol1:intro:open_license|Open License]] - By releasing everything we create under an open license
  * [[:tr:ta:vol1:intro:gl_strategy|Gateway Languages Strategy]] - By making Biblical content available to translate from a known language

==== What ====

What do we do?

  * **Open-licensed content**  - We create and make available for translation free and unrestricted biblical content
      * **[[:en:obs|Open Bible Stories]]**- a chronological mini-Bible comprising 50 key stories of the Bible, from Creation to Revelation, for evangelism and discipleship, in print, audio, and video
      * **[[:en:bible:intro|the Bible]]**- the only inspired, inerrant, sufficient, authoritative Word of God made available under an open license for unrestricted translation, use, and distribution
      * **translationNotes**  - linguistic, cultural, and exegetical helps created for translators. They exist for [[:en:obs:notes|Open Bible Stories]] and for [[:en:bible:notes:home|the Bible]].
      * **translationQuestions**  - questions for each chunk of text that the translators and checkers can ask to help insure that the translation is being understood correctly. Available for [[:en:obs:notes:questions:home|Open Bible Stories]] and for [[:en:bible:questions:home|the Bible]].
      * **[[:en:obe:home|translationWords]]**- a list of important Biblical terms with a short explanation, cross references, and translation aids. Useful for Open Bible Stories and the Bible.
  * **Tools**  - We create translation, checking, and distribution tools (all of these tools are explained in more detail in the [[:tr:ta:vol1:tech:toc_techvol1|Technology Manual]])
      * **Door43**  - an online translation platform where people can collaborate on translation and checking, also the content management system for unfoldingWord
      * **[[:tr:ta:vol1:tech:ts_intro|translationStudio]]**- a mobile app where translators can do offline translating
      * **[[:tr:ta:vol1:tech:tk_intro|translationKeyboard]]**- a mobile app where users can create custom keyboards for languages not supported by their mobile device
      * **[[:tr:ta:vol1:tech:uw_app|unfoldingWord app]]**- a mobile app where checked unfoldingWord translations are published and distributed
      * **Digital Publishing Engine**  - a system of pushing checked translations to the unfoldingWord website and app and creating print-ready documents of the content
      * **Audio Recording**  - a list of recommendations for audio recording equipment, software, and recording procedures
      * **Video Render Engine**  - an automatic video render system that takes audio recordings and merges them with video clips to create custom videos for certain projects
  * **Training**  - We create resources to train mother tongue translation teams (the training course is called [[https://unfoldingword.org/academy|translationAcademy]])
