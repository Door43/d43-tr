=== Set up the app ===

  - First you will need to [[tr:ta:vol1:tech:ts_install|install translationStudio]] on an Android device. (See also https://unfoldingword.org/translationstudio/ ))
  - [[tr:ta:vol1:tech:ts_first|First time use]] of translationStudio will require an agreement to the [[tr:ta:vol1:intro:statement_of_faith]], the [[tr:ta:vol1:intro:translation_guidelines]], and the [[tr:ta:vol1:intro:open_license]]. 

=== Set up the source language ===
