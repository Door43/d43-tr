==== Formal / Informal ====

Some languages also have different forms of "you" to indicate respect or social distance. They use the formal form of "you" when addressing someone who is an elder or respected or in authority. They use the informal "you" when talking with a peer or someone with less authority. Because the Bible was written in languages that do not have this difference, the translator will need to decide when it is best to use formal or informal forms of the language.
