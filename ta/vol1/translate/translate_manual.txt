==== What Does the Translation Manual Teach? ====

This manual teaches translation theory and how to make a good translation. Many of the modules will be immensely helpful to study before starting a translation project. Other modules are only needed for "just-in-time" learning.

Some highlights in the Translation Manual:

  * [[tr:ta:vol1:translate:choose_team]] - some items to consider before starting a translation project
